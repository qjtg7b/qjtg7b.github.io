Where I'm From

Here is some information about my hometown.

I was born in St. Louis, but I live in the suburb of St. Charles, Missouri. It is located about 25 minutes west of downtown STL. It has a population of about 66,000 people. It is known for its historic downtown location, which features a museum about the Lewis and Clark expedition. Lindenwood University, known for its hockey program, is located in St. Charles.
